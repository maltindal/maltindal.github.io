Construction of finite fields

Let 𝔽p＝ℤ/pℤ be a finite field, and let char(𝔽)＝p be the characteristic of 𝔽p, where p is prime. Let f ∈ 𝔽p[T] be an irreducible polynomial of degree n. Then 𝔽p[T]/(f) is a finite field with pn elements, where (f) is the ideal generated by f. See definitions, properties and examples.
